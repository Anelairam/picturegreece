About us

Have you ever though how would it feel not to be the only person who stays up until 12am or wakes up at 10pm and drive 50km away from home just to get the right light for your photo? Have you ever thought how would it feel not to be alone standing in the middle of nowhere for hours just to get the a beautiful photograph with lightnings heating random spots on the ground. Well the same happened to us, we are 3 people who love photography but always got this awkward feeling sometimes when everyone else was just passing by and we were just stack in the exact spot for minutes or even hours that we wanted to capture. Some people call us crazy, but we take that as a compliment as it shows the passion that we have about what we are doing. Well if you just want get rid of this awkward feeling and get to socialise with people with the same interest then…you are in the right place. We arrange several times per year and season trips around Greece in groups to capture the beauty of our land, learn new techniques and tricks on how to take the best photo ever based on the weather, the scenery the time and the season. It’s a great opportunity for everyone who is interested in photography and nature, who loves to share knowledge with the others and gain extra experience from the others.
